DashboardÂ¶

BNDL hosts a modest dashboard from the driver. It is accessible at port 8080 (or a higher port below 8100 if 8080 is not available), i.e.: http://localhost:8080/.

The dashboard is build up out of modules; one for bndl.net and bndl.execute are available by default. Others may be added by plugins, e.g. bndl_cassandra.
